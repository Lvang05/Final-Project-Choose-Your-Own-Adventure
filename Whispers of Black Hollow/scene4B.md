
# 🌑 SCENE 4B - No Light
You leave the lantern behind
and walk into the dark.

The forest falls silent.
You can’t see your hands-
only shifting shapes in the black.

Ahead, something sways.
You blink.
It’s gone.

Then…
a breath behind you.
